Training Materials and Webinars

Below you can find courses and webinars aimed for researchers and scientists to gain the technical computational skills and confidence to analyse datasets such as those generated by HoloFood. 
2nd HoloFood training workshop

Materials (lectures and exercises) for the second HoloFood training workshop held in September 2022, Bilbao, Spain "Organisation and utilisation of hologenomic datasets".

Course: Metagenomics Bioinformatics

A series of videos and supporting materials from an in-person course run by MGnify. This covers the background to the field, as well as the approaches utilised by MGnify in the analysis of data including quality control, taxonomic and functional annotation, assembly and binning, and comparative metagenomics.
