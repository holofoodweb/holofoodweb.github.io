2022 Events

1

31st Oct - 4th November 2022

Online: Genome-resolved metagenomics bioinformatics course

HoloFood partners at EMBL-EBI were teaching on the Genome resolved metagenomics bionformatics course. Learn about the tools, processes, and analysis approaches used in the field of genome-resolved metagenomics. This course will cover the use of publicly available resources to manage, share, analyse, and interpret metagenomics data, focussing primarily on assembly-based approaches. The virtually-delivered content involved participants learning via pre- recored lectures and live presentations, followed by live Q&As with the trainers. Practical experience can be developed in group activities and in computational exercises run using containerised tools on our virtual training infrastructure. Access the course materials here.
